Exercise / Workout

Regular exercise is essential for maintaining a healthy weight, improving cardiovascular fitness, and boosting your energy levels. Whether you’re just starting or looking to enhance your current routine, the right exercises can help you meet your fitness goals faster.

Benefits of Regular Exercise

  • Burns calories and helps maintain a healthy weight.
  • Strengthens muscles and bones.
  • Improves heart and lung health.
  • Reduces stress, anxiety, and improves mood.
  • Increases stamina, flexibility, and overall well-being.

Types of Workouts

Home Workouts (Beginner-Friendly)


  • Walking or jogging in place
  • Jumping jacks
  • Bodyweight squats
  • Push-ups / wall push-ups
  • Yoga stretches

Gym / Intermediate Workouts


  • Treadmill running
  • Cycling
  • Resistance training (machines or dumbbells)
  • Core workouts (planks, crunches)

Advanced / High-Intensity Workouts


  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
  • CrossFit
  • Sprint intervals
  • Weighted compound exercises (deadlifts, squats)
